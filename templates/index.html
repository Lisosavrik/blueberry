What is Spaced Repetitions?

Spaced Repetitions is a powerful and scientifically-proven learning technique that optimizes the way your brain retains information. The concept is simple yet incredibly effective: instead of cramming information all at once, our platform intelligently schedules the review of material at strategic intervals. This systematic approach allows you to remember information for the long term with minimal effort.
